About Us

We at PneuMe aim to provide a pneumonia detection service to hospitals and patients. What we do is that we predict if the patient has pneumonia or not with just a chest CT scan of the patient’s chest. Covid-19 have by far been a dominant factor in affecting people’s lungs, also causing pneumonia in majority of those cases. What most doctors suggest is that the quicker we diagnose that the lungs are being affected, the easier it is for the patient to be healthy again. With our service the primary opinion on the status of the lungs is just a click away.
